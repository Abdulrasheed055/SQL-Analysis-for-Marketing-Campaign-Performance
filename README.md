#  Project Overview

Marketing teams invest significant budgets across multiple advertising channels to acquire customers and increase conversions. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to determine which campaigns generate the highest returns and where marketing resources should be allocated.

This project analyzes a marketing campaign dataset using **Microsoft SQL Server** to evaluate campaign effectiveness, customer engagement, advertising performance, and return on investment (ROI). The analysis provides actionable insights that can help marketing managers optimize campaign strategies and improve overall marketing efficiency.

---

#  Business Problem

The marketing department wanted answers to several key business questions:

- Which campaigns generate the highest return on investment?
- Which locations receive the greatest advertising exposure?
- Which customer segments engage most with campaigns?
- Which marketing channels drive the highest conversions?
- Are advertising costs being used efficiently?
- How effective are campaigns at generating clicks?

Without these insights, marketing budgets may be allocated inefficiently, reducing overall campaign performance.

# 📝 SQL Queries & Results

This project uses SQL to answer key business questions and transform raw marketing data into actionable insights.

---

## 1️⃣ Total Impressions by Campaign

### Business Question

Which marketing campaigns generated the highest audience reach?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT
    Campaign_ID,
    SUM(Impressions) AS Total_Impressions
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
GROUP BY Campaign_ID;
```

### Result

Calculated the total number of impressions generated by every campaign.

### Business Insight

Campaigns with the highest impressions achieved the greatest brand visibility, increasing the likelihood of customer engagement and conversions.

---

## 2️⃣ Campaign with the Highest ROI

### Business Question

Which campaign delivered the best return on marketing investment?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT TOP 1
    Campaign_ID,
    Company,
    ROI
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
ORDER BY ROI DESC;
```

### Result

Campaign **ID 168** recorded the highest Return on Investment (ROI).

### Business Insight

This campaign represents the most profitable marketing initiative and provides a benchmark for future campaigns.

---

## 3️⃣ Top Three Locations by Impressions

### Business Question

Which geographic locations generated the highest advertising exposure?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT TOP 3
    Location,
    SUM(Impressions) AS Total_Impressions
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
GROUP BY Location
ORDER BY Total_Impressions DESC;
```

### Result

Top-performing locations:

- New York
- Miami
- Chicago

### Business Insight

These markets have the highest campaign visibility and represent strong opportunities for future marketing investment.

---

## 4️⃣ Average Engagement Score by Target Audience

### Business Question

Which customer demographics engage most with marketing campaigns?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT
    Target_Audience,
    AVG(Engagement_Score) AS Average_Engagement
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
GROUP BY Target_Audience
ORDER BY Average_Engagement DESC;
```

### Result

Highest engagement was recorded among:

- Women (35–44)
- Women (25–34)
- Men (25–34)
- Men (18–24)
- All Ages

### Business Insight

These customer segments respond most positively to marketing campaigns and should remain priority audiences for future campaigns.

---

## 5️⃣ Overall Click-Through Rate (CTR)

### Business Question

How effective are campaigns at converting impressions into clicks?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT
    (SUM(Clicks) * 100.0 / SUM(Impressions)) AS Overall_CTR
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$;
```

### Result

Calculated the overall Click-Through Rate (CTR) across all campaigns.

### Business Insight

CTR is a key marketing KPI that measures customer interest and campaign effectiveness.

---

## 6️⃣ Most Cost-Effective Campaign

### Business Question

Which campaign acquired customers at the lowest cost?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT
    Campaign_ID,
    Company,
    Acquisition_Cost AS Cost_Per_Conversion
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
ORDER BY Cost_Per_Conversion ASC;
```

### Result

Identified the campaign with the lowest customer acquisition cost.

### Business Insight

Lower acquisition costs improve marketing efficiency and maximize overall campaign profitability.

---

## 7️⃣ Campaigns with CTR Above 5%

### Business Question

Which campaigns achieved above-average customer engagement?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT
    Campaign_ID,
    Company,
    (Clicks * 100.0 / Impressions) AS CTR
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
WHERE (Clicks * 100.0 / Impressions) > 5;
```

### Result

Returned campaigns with a Click-Through Rate greater than **5%**.

### Business Insight

These campaigns demonstrated stronger customer engagement and can serve as best-practice examples for future marketing initiatives.

---

## 8️⃣ Marketing Channels Ranked by Conversions

### Business Question

Which marketing channels generate the highest conversions?

### SQL Query

```sql
SELECT
    Channel_Used,
    SUM(Conversion_Rate) AS Total_Conversions
FROM marketing_campaign_dataset$
GROUP BY Channel_Used
ORDER BY Total_Conversions DESC;
```

### Result

Marketing channels ranked by conversion performance:

| Rank | Channel |
|------|----------|
|  1 | Email |
|  2 | Google Ads |
|  3 | Website |
| 4 | YouTube |
| 5 | Instagram |
| 6 | Facebook |

### Business Insight

Email marketing generated the highest number of conversions, making it the most effective channel for customer acquisition in this analysis.
